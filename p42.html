Sunflowe:

Sunflowers plants display a behavior called heliotropism. The flower buds and young blossoms will face east in the morning
and follow the sun as the earth moves during the day.The Chinese associate sunflowers with long life, good luck, and happiness.
They often give these flowers as gifts on birthdays and other special occasions. In Victorian England, sunflowers symbolized
loyalty, longevity, and adoration. They were often used in floral arrangements for weddings and other celebrations.
